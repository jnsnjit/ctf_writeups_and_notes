## Reporting and Documentation ##
- The Report is the Deliverable!

1. come up with own list of findings
2. explain to non technical audience
3. priority with fixing
4. verify findings

### General Structure ###
- Executive Information
where to put resources, not technical, 3-6 sig findings, root causes and recommendations. 
leave positive messaging as well. 
- Intro
overview of test, range of devices, timeline, name and point of contact, employee who supported the pen-test, any 3rd parties. 
- Findings 
High-Low ordering of findings, show ease of exploit+impact, label vulnerable systems/objects, one-two liners of business risk, give mitigatation recommendations (more solutions are better! short term + long term)
Screenshots --> can help highlight flaws
Recommendations --> talk with client, get the root cause issue ... show ways to validate and verify that fixes are implemented
- Methodology
LONGER (10-50) pages <br>
explain process used with step by step findings <br>

- Appendix
Longer / awkward data to include in a report: optional, long form content, graphs, trusts, vulnerability scans, or comms with a third party

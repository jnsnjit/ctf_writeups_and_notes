# Cybersecurity Basics #
Security+ Recap! <br>

__CIA Triad:__
- Confidentiality: data is private, and should only be accessible for those who need it
- Integrity: data is trusted and not altered or deleted
- Availability: data is accessible when is needed to be accessed

__Red Team:__ offensive side of security, practice of testing and finding vulnerabilties in a network <br>
__Blue Team:__ defensive side of security, in charge of properly configured a network's security, monitoring network activity, etc ... <br>
__Purple Team:__ mix of both <br>

_Black V Grey V White:_
- Black: malicious threat actors
- Grey: unauthorized actors that may act with good intentions
- White: Ethical Hackers, bug bounty hunters, people with allow authorization to test vulnerabilities for a company

__Ethics:__
- It is in the name of the class, and it is important to realize that there can be a lot of legal repocussion for malicious actions done a network, regardless of its intended result. 
- Paperwork and Documentation: everything has to be documentation when and before it is done, and this is to both protect you as the ethical hacker, and also protect the company requesting your service.
- Rules of Engagement - what is allowed and what is not allowed in a test
